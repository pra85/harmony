Your music in the same place

Harmony natively supports SoundCloud, Spotify, Google Play Music, Deezer, Hype Machine and even your disk files

Coverflow (beta)

Coverflow allows you to have a preview on your library in an unique way - enable it in the settings

Dark mode

Use Cmd+D to switch to dark mode

Responsive

Whatever size you want it to be, it will follow you

Sound menu integration

Use the mini player in the system sound indicator to control your music without interrupting what you're doing

Media keys and shortcut

Support for native media keys & keyboard shortcuts

Notification

Be notified whenever a new track starts playing

Last.fm

Harmony natively supports Last.fm scrobbling
